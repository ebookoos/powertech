About Us

Who we are

PowerTech is a member of the Venture Garden Group; the largest FinTech investment holding company in Sub-Sahara Africa. PowerTech is a utility sector focused Nigerian-based technology firm that works with strategic partners in both private and public institutions in emerging markets to provide fit for purpose solutions to business challenges.


PowerTech as a FinTech company, has designed, implemented and continues to help industry leaders to transform the Power sector. We are able to achieve this through innovative technology design for data capture, revenue assurance in collections and payment, process automation, real-time visibility, data analytics and IT consultation for effective policy development and execution.

PowerTech has the capability to meet the challenges of a changing electricity landscape and also shape the future of the sector by impacting:

Revenue loss prevention through automation and transparency

Elimination of financial waste

Providing data for impactful governance
