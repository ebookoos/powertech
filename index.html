Who We Are

PowerTech is a member of Venture Garden Group; the largest FinTech investment holding company in Sub-Saharan Africa. PowerTech is a utility sector-focused Nigerian-based technology firm that works with strategic partners in both private and public institutions in emerging markets to provide fit for purpose solutions to business challenges..


PowerTech has designed, implemented and continues to help industry leaders to transform the Power sector. We are able to achieve this through innovative technology design for data capture, revenue assurance in collections and payment, process automation, real-time visibility, data analytics and IT consultation for effective policy development and execution.

Our Products

  • Energy Monitoring
  • Revenue Assurance
  • Maximum Demand Customer Metering
  • Energy Asset and Concession Management
  • Business Intelligence

PowerTech’s energy monitoring solution provides utilities with the opportunity to remotely and proactively manage their customer’s energy consumption. With features such as direct debit, Geographical Information Systems (GIS), central billing platform and remote meter reading, our energy monitoring system is a robust solution that ensures optimum energy usage.

Our revenue assurance solution helps our customers transform their revenue collection processes and operations with innovative strategies aligned to specific needs and unique organizational requirements.

PowerTech’s maximum demand energy metering solution provides distribution companies with the capability to manage customers with high energy usage remotely thereby saving administrative costs.

Our software solution assists our customers with energy asset monitoring and multiple concessionaires’ management to enhance effectiveness, efficiency, savings and service delivery.

PowerTech’s Business Intelligence solution is designed specifically to provide insights using organization’s data for improved and data driven decision making. The solution provides organizations with a common view of its business data from disparate systems providing a simplified approach to data standardization, aggregation, analysis and presentation. It grants executives and decision makers’ visibility into the performance and health of their business based on their data.
